## Impact on Software Development Organizations
- **SDLC Integration Requirements:** Organizations must integrate security practices from SP 800-218 and SP 800-218A into their Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC) to ensure robust security measures are in place.
- **Supply Chain Security Mandates:** The guidelines from SP 800-204D necessitate that development teams incorporate supply chain security practices into their CI/CD pipelines, addressing vulnerabilities in third-party components.
- **Compliance Requirements:** Updates to SP 800-171 Rev. 3 emphasize the need for compliance with CMMC and FedRAMP, mandating that controlled unclassified information is protected throughout its lifecycle.
- **AI/ML Security Considerations:** The introduction of SP 800-218A highlights the importance of addressing security concerns specific to generative AI, requiring development teams to adapt their practices accordingly.
